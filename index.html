Gunpla - My Favorite Hobby

What is Gunpla?

Gunpla, or Gundam Plastic Models, are model kits manufactured by Bandai. These kits allow hobbyists to build detailed replicas of Mobile Suits from the Gundam franchise.

Why I Love Gunpla

Gunpla allows me to be creative, stay focused, and appreciate the articulation and detail in each model. I enjoy customizing my kits through painting, panel lining, and applying decals.
